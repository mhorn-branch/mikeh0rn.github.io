What is Pair Programming?

Collaborative problem solving to achieve programming goals through communicating the objective at hand and executing the strategy together.

Why is Pair Programming important to DBC?

Pair programming is important to DBC because it helps effectively prepare students for teamwork in real life environments. Through effective pair programming we grow and learn together by speaking through problems and collaboratively finding the best strategy to approach all portions of the challenge. at hand

Compare driver and navigator

While the Driver takes lead on the coding and syntax, the Navigator focuses on logic and WHY we are approaching a challenge a certain way. The driver is not meant to be a parrot of the navigator but more of the point person to translate and implement the thoughts of the Nagivator. When necessary, the driver will step in to provide feedback or push back on a particular approach.
